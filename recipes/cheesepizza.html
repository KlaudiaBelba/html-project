Cheese Pizza

Description

There are days when I crave for cheese that I put cheese on everything!
One time I made some egg sandwiches and I added grated cheese on it.
Remember the Tteokbokki recipe I made? The dish tasted so much better with melted cheese on top!
Let’s satisfy our pizza and cheese cravings with this easy Four Cheese Pizza recipe!

Now that you know all about the different types of cheese, let’s get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp olive oil additional
  • 3 tbsp softened unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves medium garlic minced
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese shredded
  • 1 cup white cheddar cheese shredded
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese shredded
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese freshly grated
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley optional

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl or mixer, combine the water, honey , and yeast. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes until yeast starts to form.

  2. Add salt, flour, and 1 tbsp olive oil. Mix well until the dough pulls away from the bowl.

  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes, manually.

  4. Form the dough into a bowl. Cover the dough with the remaining olive oil, place back into the bowl, and cover with a damp towel. Place in a warm area and let the dough rise for about 45 minutes.

  5. Preheat oven to 450°F. If you have a pizza pan, lightly oil it with olive oil. Otherwise, you can use a sheet of parchment paper placed on top of the baking pan or cooling rack.

  6. Flatten the dough and lay it on top of the pizza pan or baking rack with parchment paper.

  7. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened butter and garlic. Spread it evenly on the pizza dough.

  8. Sprinkle all the cheeses, evenly, except the parmesan.

  9. Bake for 13 to 15 minutes until the crust is golden brown. Sprinkle with fresh parmesan and parsley.
